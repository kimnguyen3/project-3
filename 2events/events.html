Events

Day 1

A focus on recycling and reusing.

Day 2

Understanding fabric and its disposal.

Day 3

Unfair labor practices and what you can do.

Day 1

Clothing Swap

Attendees are encouraged to bring 3 - 5 items of clothing they already own that are in good condition but they no longer wear. Participants will then be grouped by size in order to find other participants to swap clothing items with. This event is easily replicated, giving attendees a method to facilitate a clothing swap within their own communities to highlight the easily cyclical nature of fashion.

Upcycling Workshop

Participants will learn various ways to upcycle clothing. Needle and thread, fabric scraps, and plain clothing items will be provided to follow along with the live tutorials. Attendees will learn how to mend holes, resize garments, and add decoration to plain pieces they may already own.

Day 2

Fabric Seminar

A seminar on what fabrics are sustainable and durable will he help to educate consumers on what to look for in stores to make a purchase worth their money. There will also be fabric samples at the end of the seminar for consumer to physically feel to get a better grasp on the differences between fabrics.

Clothing Canyon

Tours will be led through an art installation replicating the mountains of textile waste that are shipped to developing countries, especially in Africa, with no designated landfills. This waste sits in people's communities creating canyons of unwanted clothing.

Day 3

"UNTOLD" Screening

"UNTOLD: Inside the SHEIN Machine", a documentary about the horrible working conditions in fast fashion factories in Southeast Asia, will be shown. A discussion/Q&A with a panel of worker's rights activists along with the creators of the documentary will follow the screening.

Call to Action

The final seminar will teach attendees ways to lobby governments to protect workers and the environment, support initiatives aiming to fix this issue, and how to speak on this issue with their own communities. This seminar will also give a platform to speakers from sustainable brands to reach a wider audience.
